## Support Overview

AssessmentTracker is an internal consulting platform developed and maintained by Peter Holdorf to support Operational Excellence Assessments (OEAF), operational discovery, reporting, and related consulting activities.

Support is provided directly by the software developer as part of authorized consulting engagements.

---

## Support Services

Support is available for:

- Questions regarding AssessmentTracker functionality
- Microsoft Graph integration
- QuickBooks Online integration
- OAuth authorization and connection issues
- Operational reporting and reconciliation features
- Software defects and unexpected behavior
- Questions regarding the End User License Agreement, Privacy Policy, or Third-Party Services documentation

## Reporting Issues

When reporting an issue, please include, when applicable:

- A description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce the issue
- Date and time the issue occurred
- Relevant error messages
- The connected service involved (Microsoft Graph, QuickBooks Online, or other supported integration)

Providing this information helps expedite troubleshooting.

---

## Third-Party Service Issues

AssessmentTracker integrates with third-party services including Microsoft Graph and QuickBooks Online.

If an issue originates with a third-party provider (for example, service outages, authentication failures, or API changes), resolution may depend upon that provider's systems and support processes.
