# AssessmentTracker Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 20, 2026

> Published from AssessmentTracker, Effective Date July 20, 2026.

---

## 1. Information Collected

AssessmentTracker is an internal consulting platform used to conduct Operational Excellence Assessments (OEAF) for individual client organizations. The Software is operated directly by the consultant with the client's explicit authorization. Clients do not self-register and do not create user accounts.

During an authorized consulting engagement, AssessmentTracker may collect and store:

- Operational and process information gathered during interviews, observations, and working sessions.
- Metadata and records retrieved from connected services (currently Microsoft Graph and QuickBooks Online), limited strictly to the information explicitly authorized through each provider's OAuth consent process.
- Workpapers, findings, reports, and other consulting artifacts produced during the engagement.

AssessmentTracker does not collect information from the general public.

---

## 2. Connected Services

AssessmentTracker currently integrates with:

- **Microsoft Graph** — Outlook calendar, email metadata, and other Microsoft 365 information relevant to operational discovery.
- **QuickBooks Online** — Accounting and invoicing information relevant to operational reporting and reconciliation support.

Additional integrations may be added in future releases as consulting requirements evolve.

---

## 3. OAuth Authentication

AssessmentTracker accesses information only after authorization by the owner of the connected account.

Authentication to Microsoft Graph is performed directly through Microsoft's OAuth authorization system.

Authentication to QuickBooks Online is performed directly through Intuit's OAuth authorization system.

AssessmentTracker never requests, collects, or stores usernames or passwords for any connected service.

OAuth access and refresh tokens are stored locally on the consultant's secured workstation solely to maintain authenticated sessions with authorized services.

Access granted to AssessmentTracker may be revoked at any time through the connected service provider (such as Microsoft or Intuit).

---

## 4. How Information Is Used

Information obtained through connected services is used solely to support the consulting engagement for which authorization was granted, including:

- Operational discovery
- Metadata collection
- Operational reporting
- Operational reconciliation support
- Preparation of consulting findings and recommendations

AssessmentTracker does not use collected information for advertising, marketing, behavioral profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the authorized engagement.

---

## 5. Data Storage

AssessmentTracker follows a local-first architecture.

OAuth access tokens, refresh tokens, retrieved metadata, workpapers, findings, reports, and related consulting artifacts are stored locally on the consultant's secured workstation unless the client explicitly authorizes another storage location.

AssessmentTracker does not maintain a centralized cloud service for client operational data.

---

## 6. Data Sharing

AssessmentTracker does not:

- Sell client information.
- Sell personal information.
- Broker client information.
- Display advertising.
- Use advertising or marketing trackers.
- Perform behavioral profiling.

Information obtained during an engagement is shared only:

- With the client's explicit authorization.
- As required by applicable law.
- As necessary to fulfill contractual obligations with the client.

---

## 7. Data Security

AssessmentTracker is designed using a least-privilege philosophy.

The Software requests only the permissions necessary to perform authorized operational discovery and reporting.

AssessmentTracker emphasizes read-oriented access and does not intentionally modify accounting or business records during assessment activities.

Any future capability that writes information back to connected systems will require separate, explicit authorization before use.

OAuth tokens and assessment data are maintained on the consultant's secured workstation rather than a shared or publicly accessible system.

---

## 8. Data Retention

Operational data retrieved during an engagement is retained only as long as reasonably necessary to support:

- Consulting workpapers
- Findings
- Reports
- Project documentation

Retention beyond the active engagement is governed by the applicable client agreement and the client's instructions.

---

## 9. User Rights

AssessmentTracker does not operate self-service customer accounts.

Requests regarding access to, correction of, or deletion of operational information collected during an engagement should be directed to the consultant identified below.

Such requests will be handled in accordance with the applicable client engagement and governing law.

This Privacy Policy applies only to AssessmentTracker's handling of information retrieved through those authorized integrations.
